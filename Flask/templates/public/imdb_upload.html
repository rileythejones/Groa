IMDB Upload

If you've used IMDb to rate movies, you can upload your ratings.csv file to Gróa to get endless custom movie recommendations.
We do not keep a copy of this data, and it is deleted from our server at the end of your session.


To get this file and upload it here, follow these steps:

1. Login to IMDb.

2. Click on your username and select "Your ratings".

3. To the right of the words "Your Ratings", you'll see three dots in a vertical line. Click that and press "Export". Save the file somewhere convenient, like your "Downloads" folder.

4. Click on the "Browse" button below, navigate to the file, and double-click to upload.

5. Finally, click "Upload File" below.
